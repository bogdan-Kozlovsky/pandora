About

Pandora Studio is one of the most ambitious projects on the Ukrainian photo market and today it is the largest photo studio in Ukraine.

Our company provides a full range of services in the field of photo and video production. A team of professionals is able to implement a project of any complexity from the formation of the idea to the final post-production.

A team of professionals ready to translate your idea into reality! No ideas? We will develop a concept and images for shooting with you. We are ready to go for experiments and hold photo shoots of any complexity.

We provide an expanded range of services for our customers. Our experts will help you set up equipment for your shooting. In each of the 7 rooms there is a complete set of equipment for studio shooting.

Shooting process

  • Photo order

    Our clients can order a photo session in two ways: by contacting the manager by phone or leaving a request on the website.

  • Material shooting

    On the set day, the client arrives at the photo session. Production coordinator or studio administrators introduce you to the team.

  • Retouching

    Production coordinator, having received a letter from the client with the selected photos, sends the order to the retoucher.

  • Sending photos

    Retouched photos are checked by the production coordinator. Further, the manager sends to the client the ordered package, which consists of two archives.
